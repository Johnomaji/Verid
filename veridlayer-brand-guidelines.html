UPPERCASE
Usage Rules
✓ Do

Use the dark navy background as default. The brand lives in dark mode. Use teal for primary actions and interactive elements. Use amber sparingly for data callouts and commodity-related visuals. Maintain generous whitespace. Let the data breathe.

✗ Don't

No generic blockchain gradients (purple/blue washes). No floating token graphics or globe illustrations. No light-mode-first designs without approval. Don't use teal and amber together in equal weight — teal leads, amber accents. Don't stretch or modify the logo mark.

✓ Photography

Use real commodity photography: cocoa beans, grain silos, warehouse interiors, port logistics, African market scenes. Apply duotone overlays (navy + teal or navy + amber) for brand consistency. Favor documentary-style images over stock photo aesthetics.

✗ Avoid

No AI-generated imagery. No generic "handshake" or "business meeting" stock photos. No clip art or illustrated icons that feel clip-art-like. No excessive use of Solana branding — reference the ecosystem, don't cosplay it.
